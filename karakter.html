Kevin Sanjaya Sukamuljo

The "Minion" King of Badminton

Indonesian badminton superstar specializing in men's doubles with his explosive playing style and incredible reflexes.

About The Minion

  • Full Name: Kevin Sanjaya Sukamuljo
  • Born: August 2, 1995 in Banyuwangi, Indonesia
  • Height: 1.69 m (5 ft 7 in) - nickname "The Minion"
  • Playing Style: Aggressive, fast, unpredictable net play
  • Current Partner: Rahmat Hidayat (since 2023)
  • Former Partner: Marcus Fernaldi Gideon (2015-2023)
  • Career High Ranking: World No. 1 in Men's Doubles
  • Playing Hand: Right-handed
  • Coach: Herry Iman Pierngadi
  • Signature Moves: Lightning-fast net play, deceptive shots
  • Team: Jaya Raya Jakarta badminton club
  • Training Base: Jakarta, Indonesia

Career Achievements

  • World No. 1 for 197 weeks with Marcus Gideon
  • Longest consecutive weeks at No. 1: 76 weeks
  • 2× All England Champion (2017, 2018)
  • Asian Games Gold Medalist (2018)
  • 11× BWF World Tour winners
  • Fastest recorded smash: 426 km/h
  • 7 Superseries titles in one year (2017)
  • BWF Best Male Player of the Year (2017)
  • SEA Games Gold Medalist (2015, 2019)
  • Asian Championships Silver (2018, 2019)
  • Indonesia Open Champion (2016, 2017, 2018, 2019)
  • China Open Champion (2016, 2017, 2018)
